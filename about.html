About Neighborhood Watch

Neighborhood Watch is a community-driven platform that empowers residents to report and track local issues such as potholes, streetlight outages, garbage concerns, and more. Our goal is to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal services by providing a simple, transparent, and collaborative reporting tool.

By fostering open communication and encouraging collective action, we aim to improve the quality of life in our neighborhoods. Whether you're reporting a problem or celebrating a community achievement, Neighborhood Watch helps make your voice heard and your neighborhood better.
